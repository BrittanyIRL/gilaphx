Why zines?

To accompany the priceless and intangible notion of communal experience, Gila will also offer you something physical, tearable, and disposable. Gathering a selection of our favorite stories and scenes from the weeks and months past, the Gila zine will be a visceral, albeit temporal medium - a reminder that we are here and then we aren’t.
